Better Together

Started more than 10 years ago, EPAAS has come a long way as a project and a viable community. Over the last year we kick started the EPAAS Foundation and the community is more growing and vibrant than ever.

Working together ultimately makes us better. Life on earth evolved from single-cell organisms to multi-celled organisms when some cells started to specialise and co-operate with each other, increasing their chance of survival and growth. Similary the EPAAS community is now coming togther, where by specialization and co-operation, all of us become better.
