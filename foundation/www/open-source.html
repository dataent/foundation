100% Open Source

Unlike other open source business platforms, EPAAS is categorically Open Source, because unlike them, we don't think sustainability and open source live at odds.

How EPAAS is Different?

Most open source platforms in the ERP / CRM / E-Commerce space are open core. These communities makes money from closed extensions and services to monetize their efforts. We believe this is not only wrong, but not sustainable. Here are the reasons

Open Core is not really Open Source

  • Closed extensions are not high quality because they have not been vetted by the core developers and maintainers.
  • Closed extensions are not easy to upgrade.
  • The community has perverse incentive not to contribute back.
  • New users feel cheated when they discover that the product is not usable as it is
  • Competing extensions end up doing the same thing, differently.

Making Open Source Work for You

We know it is hard to make money from open source directly, but we have very high standards. The core maintainers of EPAAS are fully committed to open source and we expect the community to also believe in this core value.

If you are a service provider, there are many creative ways you can make money from EPAAS without diluting the open source imperative.

If you are an end user, don't fall prey to closed extensions, they are a short cut that will ultimately end up costing you a significant more time and money.
